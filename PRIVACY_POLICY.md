Privacy Policy for Arya Translate
Last updated: 2026.6.11
Arya Translate respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII).
Data We Access:
The extension only accesses the text content of the web pages you actively choose to translate. This content is sent directly to our translation API provider solely for the purpose of translation and is not stored on our servers.
Local Storage:
User preferences (such as target language and UI settings) are stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API and are never transmitted externally.
Third-Party Services:
We use qwen MT,DeepSeek mimopro for translation. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle data.
